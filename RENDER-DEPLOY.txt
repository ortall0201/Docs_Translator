STEP 1: DEPLOY BACKEND ON RENDER
=================================
1. Go to https://render.com
2. Sign in with GitHub (connect ortall0201/Docs_Translator repo)
3. Click "New" â†’ "Web Service"
4. Select "Deploy an existing image from a registry"
5. Image URL: ortalyy/docs4u-backend:latest
6. Service Name: docs4u-backend
7. Region: Choose closest to you
8. Instance Type: Free (or paid for better performance)
9. Port: 8000
10. Environment Variables:
    - Key: OPENAI_API_KEY
    - Value: [YOUR_OPENAI_API_KEY_FROM_BACKEND_.env_FILE]
11. Click "Deploy"
